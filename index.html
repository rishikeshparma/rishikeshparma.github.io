About Me

I completed my PhD in Operations Management at the University of Luxembourg. My research focuses on optimization under uncertainty, with applications in mechanism design and revenue management. In my dissertation, I develop analytical models and design algorithms to address resource allocation problems in uncertain environments. My doctoral work is supervised by Prof. Çağıl Koçyiğit and Prof. Benny Mantin. During my PhD, I was a visiting researcher at Imperial College London, hosted by Prof. Wolfram Wiesemann.

I hold an Integrated Master’s degree in Mathematics and Computing from IIT Dhanbad (2019).
